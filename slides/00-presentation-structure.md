# Spec-Driven Development Presentation Structure

## Target Audience
IT specialists (developers, architects, QAs, product managers)

## Duration
45-60 minutes (including Q&A)

## Presentation Flow

### Part 1: Introduction to Spec-Driven Development (10-15 min)
- Slide 1: Title & Overview
- Slide 2-3: The Evolution of Software Development
- Slide 4-5: What is Spec-Driven Development?
- Slide 6: SDD vs Traditional Development
- Slide 7: Why SDD Matters Now

### Part 2: Introducing Spec-Kit (10 min)
- Slide 8: What is Spec-Kit?
- Slide 9: Core Philosophy & Principles
- Slide 10: Development Phases (Greenfield, Creative, Brownfield)
- Slide 11: The 6-Step Workflow Overview

### Part 3: Command-by-Command Deep Dive (25-30 min)
- Slide 12: Setup & Installation
- Slide 13-15: `/speckit.constitution` - Establish Principles
- Slide 16-19: `/speckit.specify` - Define Requirements
- Slide 20-22: `/speckit.clarify` - Refine Specifications
- Slide 23-26: `/speckit.plan` - Create Technical Plan
- Slide 27-29: `/speckit.analyze` - Validate the Plan
- Slide 30-33: `/speckit.tasks` - Generate Task Breakdown
- Slide 34-37: `/speckit.implement` - Execute Implementation

### Part 4: Best Practices & Wrap-up (5-10 min)
- Slide 38: Success Stories & Metrics
- Slide 39: Common Pitfalls & How to Avoid Them
- Slide 40: Getting Started - Next Steps
- Slide 41: Q&A
